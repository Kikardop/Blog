Discover how I started playing padel, what makes this sport so exciting, and my experiences in competitions and events.

How It All Started

Three years ago, I picked up a padel racket for the first time. What started as a casual game with friends quickly turned into a passion.

What I Love About Padel

The combination of strategy, teamwork, and quick reflexes makes every match an exhilarating experience. I enjoy every moment on the court!

My Padel Timeline

  • 2021: Joined a local padel club and began training.
  • 2022: Competed in the Regional Padel Championship.
  • 2023: Achieved runner-up in the Summer Padel Cup.
